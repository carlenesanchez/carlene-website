a little about
who i am

I'm a Stanford MS&E grad and Strategy Consultant at Deloitte, working at the intersection of enterprise AI and the human side of change — i.e., how organizations actually adopt new technology, not just implement it. I've concentrated heavily in Deloitte's change management practice, reflecting my passion for building culture, people readiness, and both the client and internal talent experience. My work spans AI-augmented training programs for Fortune 500 clients, custom Claude workflows, and GTM/operations for Deloitte's Human Capital AI portfolio.

In my free time, I host free apartment cafés and events under my pseudonym celerysjoe, as well as a "low-stakes" book club (add me on Goodreads!). I genuinely believe people are hungry for connection in this unprecedented age of isolation — and we're only just beginning to build the tools and spaces to meet that need.
